About Hope Hands Foundation

Welcome to Hope Hands Foundation, founded in 2015 in Johannesburg. We are a community-based charity offering shelter, food, and job training for the homeless and disadvantaged. We began with giving food to the homeless and now operate two community centres and weekly outreach programs.

To give back to the community and to be able to provide to those who cannot provide for themselves.

A place where no one has to struggle for basic needs and to have a community where everyone is happy.

  • Homeless individuals in rural areas
  • Volunteers and donors
  • Local government and NGOs
