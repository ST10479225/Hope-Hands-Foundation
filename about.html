Hope Hands Foundation

Founded in 2015 in Johannesburg, Hope Hands Foundation is a community-based charity offering shelter, food, and job training for the homeless and disadvantaged. The organization began with food outreach for the homeless and now operates two community centres and weekly outreach programs.

Mission Statement

To give back to the community and to be able to provide to those who cannot provide for themselves.

Vision Statement

A place where no one has to struggle for basic needs and a community where everyone is happy.

Target Audience

  • Homeless individuals in rural areas
  • Volunteers and donors
  • Local government and NGOs

Website Goals and Objectives

Goals

  • Increase monthly donations by 50% within 6 months
  • Streamline volunteer sign-up and scheduling
  • Spread awareness through posts and events

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Online donations per month
  • New volunteer sign-ups
  • Engagement on vlog videos and social media platforms
  • Bounce rate on homepage
