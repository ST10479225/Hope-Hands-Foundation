Hope Hands Foundation

Welcome to Hope Hands Foundation, the Founded in 2015 in Johannessburg, HopeHands Foundation is a community-based charity offering shelter, food, and job training for the homeless and disadvantaged. They began with giving food to the homeless and now operate two community centres and weekly outreach programs.

Mission statement

To give back to the community and to be able to provide to those who cannot provide for themselves

Vision statement

A place where no one has to struggle for basic needs and to have a community where everyone is happy.

Target Audience

  • Homeless individuals in rural areas
  • Volunteers and donors
  • Local government and NGOs
